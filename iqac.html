Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

Systematizing and professionalizing quality enhancement measures at MCT College of Legal Studies.

Vision and Mission

"To ensure a quality-centric academic environment and to stimulate the academic environment for promotion of quality of teaching-learning and research in higher education institutions."

Major Functions

  • Development and application of quality benchmarks/parameters.
  • Facilitating the creation of a learner-centric environment conducive to quality education.
  • Arrangement for feedback response from students, parents and other stakeholders.

MCT College of Legal Studies is a premier centre of legal education in South Malabar, committed to quality and affordable legal education.
