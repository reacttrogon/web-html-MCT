Academics

Admission, programmes, and student-focused initiatives.

Admission Requirements

Required Original Certificates at the time of admission:

  • Passing Certificate / Degree Certificate.
  • Transfer certificate issued from the Institution last studied.
  • Conduct and Character Certificate from the Institution last studied.
  • Migration Certificate: Graduates from universities other than University of Calicut must attach the original Migration Certificate.
  • Eligibility / Equivalency Certificate.

Quota & Norms

The admissions to the 50% Govt. quota will be as per Govt./University norms and Management quota as per Management norms.

Tuition Fee

Tuition fee and other fees will be as prescribed by the Government from time to time.

General Discipline

  • Discipline is highly essential for the smooth working of the college. Each student shall maintain it at all times.
  • Students shall always behave with dignity and courtesy. Absolute obedience is expected from every student.
  • Smoking is strictly prohibited in the college campus (Cir.No.35362/08/H&FWD, dated 22/09/08).
  • Political activities are strictly banned inside the campus. No student shall involve in any political activity.

Legal Education at MCT CLS

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome new students, parents and family members to our prestigious institution MCT College of Legal Studies. I am highly thankful to them for endowing such high confidence on our institution. As the proverb states, "well begun is half done," you have taken that first step in making law your career.

The prime object of legal education is the production of professional lawyers, judicial officers, academicians, and legal officers for various multinational companies. At MCT CLS, we aim to inculcate students with both substantive and procedural legal rules.

"MCT CLS was started in the year 2015 with a noble aim to provide legal education to the downtrodden and needy in and around Kerala, especially North Malabar."

Presently, the College offers the 5 Year integrated BBA LL.B (Hons.) Course and the 3 Year LL.B Course, each with an intake of 50 students. Modern legal education is a blend of both theory and practice. To achieve this, the college provides clinical legal education and encourages Moot Court competitions and mock trials.

We emphasize both curricular and extra-curricular activities. Our team of dedicated, qualified, and experienced staff strive to provide students with every opportunity to achieve their goals.

Finishing School Programme

I would like to welcome our professional students and take this opportunity to thank you for showing your interest in our academic and professional programmes in legal studies. Our institution is strongly committed and determined towards academic excellence and professional growth.

From our end, we aim to provide the finest environment possible for teaching and learning. We teach programmes backed by our highly qualified and experienced teachers. This provides the scope to give students the opportunity to achieve their full potential in legal studies.

Students Grievance Redressal

To achieve this vision, the college strives to impact comprehensive legal education by creating an environment in which every student could discover and realize his potential to the fullest extent.

MCT College of Legal Studies is established with the main objective of imparting quality-based professional education to the students coming from all strata of the society and it aims at securing the following goals:

  • To evolve and impart comprehensive legal education at all levels to achieve excellence.
  • To raise legal awareness in community for a greater social and economic justice.
  • Combining quality legal education along with necessary input of practical training.
  • To equip students for a variety of legal and law-related career paths.
  • To inculcate a positive attitude and a sense of true professionalism among the students and make them self-reliant.

National Seminars & Workshops

"Law is an instrument of social change."

In a democratic country like India, the relevancy of legal education is growing day by day. Knowledge of law increases one's understanding of public affairs. Its study promotes accuracy of expression, facility in arguments, and skill in interpreting written words, as well as an understanding of social values.

It is the pivotal duty of everyone to know the law. Ignorance of law is not innocence but a sin which cannot be excused. MCT College of Legal Studies is dedicated to advancing human dignity, social welfare, and justice through knowledge of law.

We aim to impart legal education which not only produces good lawyers but creates cultured, law-abiding citizens inculcated with concepts of human values and human rights. Our graduates can serve humanity in various capacities such as administrators, law teachers, jurists, judges, and industrial entrepreneurs.

Achievements & Goals

The College aims to be a premier academic institution in providing quality and affordable legal education. At present, the courses offered by the College are the Five Year integrated BBA-LL.B (Hons) and the Three Year LL.B.

Our students have consistently shown excellence in moot courts, legal aid drives, and academic performance, setting a benchmark for legal studies in the North Malabar region.

MCT College of Legal Studies is a premier centre of legal education in South Malabar, committed to quality and affordable legal education.
